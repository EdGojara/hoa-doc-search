Annual Meeting Setup

Configure quorum threshold, meeting officials, and location. The check-in app reads these values when generating the quorum-evidence PDF.

1. Pick the election

Lists active elections from the voting platform. Pick the one for the meeting you're setting up.
Loading elections…

2. Meeting details

Pre-populated from the election record where possible. Edit as needed.

3. Quorum threshold

The percentage of voting units required for the meeting to transact business. Pulled from your Bylaws — usually under "Quorum" or "Annual Meeting."
Enter total voting units + quorum % to see the calculated threshold.

4. Meeting officials

For the signature block on the quorum-evidence PDF.

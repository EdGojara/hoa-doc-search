Where this shows up

Cascading from lib/brand.js + public/brand.css (single sources of truth), every customer-facing surface picks up the new identity automatically.

  • Every page in public/ — admin tools, portal, login
  • Every server-rendered PDF — builder letters, violation letters, postcards, AR reminders, board packets
  • Email templates with brand signatures
  • The printable submission form (/august-meadows-submission-form.html)
  • Server-generated PDF version (/forms/august-meadows-submission.pdf)

Historical artifacts already in library_documents (signed board minutes, past violation letters) stay as-is — not retroactively rebranded. Going-forward renders use the new identity.

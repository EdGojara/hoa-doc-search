The three voice registers
Engagement

Warm, community, social

Community events, surveys, newsletters, board candidate spotlights, social.

  • Hero photo, multiple CTAs
  • "Director of Fun"-style sender
  • Forward-to-neighbor mechanic
  • Per-CTA click tracking
Operational

Clear, actionable, professional

Meeting reminders, maintenance notices, hurricane prep, schedule changes.

  • Cleaner layout, text-forward
  • Single primary CTA
  • Calendar-friendly
  • Required-deliverable status tracked
Compliance

Formal, legally precise, branded

Violations, fines, §209 cure notices, statutory mailings.

  • Statutory wording from GLOBAL_RULES
  • Auto-routes to certified mail (Lob) when law requires
  • Audit trail per recipient
  • Cannot be unsubscribed by homeowner
How a blast moves through the system
1

Author + draft with Claire

Staff opens Compose, picks register, describes goal in plain English. Claire drafts in the community's voice preset. Staff reviews, edits, previews on desktop + mobile.

2

Segmentation + compliance gates

Audience built via community / section / property type / role / interest opt-in. CAN-SPAM footer auto-injected. Compliance category cannot be unsubscribed.

3

Deliver via Resend

Community-branded sender domain. Per-recipient delivery row created. Send-time throttling. Statutory mailings that legally require certified mail re-route to Lob automatically.

4

Track everything

Opens, clicks per CTA, bounces, unsubscribes, forwards, replies. Per-blast view + per-template performance + per-community baselines + portfolio view.

5

Replies feed messaging — not /dev/null

When a homeowner replies, the message lands in the trustEd messaging inbox as an inbound thread anchored to their property. Vantaca suppresses replies. We treat them as the highest-value engagement signal.
